"Art Gallery -- Exhibition erverywhere"

"Art Gallery -- Exhibition erverywhere"
is a 3D art gallery where images and 3D models can be displayed.
The gallery is a WebGL application created with Babylon.js. The models are created using Blender. The user can look around the gallery, move around and view the images or models. By clicking on the viewed image, information about the particular artwork can be displayed.

Work in progress!
Future implementations could also include a simple exhibition management system that allows administrators to add, remove and organize images and models.
The project has many possibilities and it can vary according to requirements and may require close collaboration with artists to ensure that the works presented are well presented.
